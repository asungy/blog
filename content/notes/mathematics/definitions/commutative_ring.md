## Definition
A [ring](./ring.md) \(R\) is said to be a **commutative ring** if \(ab = ba\) for all \(a,b \in R\).

## Example
Axiom: \(\mathbb{Z},\; \mathbb{Q},\; \mathbb{R}, \text{ and } \mathbb{C}\) are all commutative rings.
