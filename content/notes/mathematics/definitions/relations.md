# Definition

Let \(S\) and \(T\) be sets. Then a **relation** from \(S\) to \(T\) is a subset \(\rho\) of \(S \times T\). If \(s \in S\) and \(t \in T\), then we write \(s \rho t\) if \((s,t) \in \rho\);
