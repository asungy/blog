## Definition
Let \(\rho\) be a relation on \(S\). We say that \(\rho\) is **reflexive** if \(a\rho a\) for all \(a \in S\).

## Example
On \(\mathbb{Z}\), the relation \(\leq\) is reflexive, but not \(\lt\) is not.
